How We Work

Three ways we work with you.

Service 01

Fractional Product Leadership

We step in as your Head of Product — part-time, embedded, and focused on what matters: product vision, strategy, and the decisions that shape your roadmap.

Best for Companies growing without senior product leadership. Founders overloaded with tactical decisions. Organizations navigating AI integration or transformation.
2–3 days per week, medium-term contracts (3–6 months)
Product vision, strategy, and outcome-driven roadmaps
Alignment across product, engineering, design, data, and business
Remote and global, B2B contractor model
Service 02

Product Discovery Sprint

A focused engagement that turns uncertainty into clear, actionable product decisions. Not research for the sake of research — discovery oriented toward executive decision-making.

Best for Product stagnation. High-stakes bets. Roadmaps driven by opinion instead of evidence.
4–6 weeks, fixed scope and fixed price
Clearly framed problems, validated hypotheses, MVP definition
Ends with executive-level decisions, not a report

Best for Companies that want AI to be a product differentiator, not just a buzzword. Teams exploring agents and automation.
